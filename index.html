Navigating Our System: Roles and Responsibilities

For Hospital Administrators

Admins are the backbone of our system, overseeing crucial operational aspects. This includes the secure **registration and management of doctor profiles**, updating hospital services, and maintaining the overall integrity and security of the digital platform. They ensure all systems run seamlessly for optimal patient care.

For Dedicated Doctors

Once officially **onboarded by an Admin**, doctors utilize their personalized portal to efficiently manage their clinical practice. This includes scheduling and conducting appointments, securely accessing comprehensive patient medical records, documenting progress, and facilitating inter-departmental consultations. Our system empowers doctors to deliver focused, high-quality care.

For Valued Patients

Patients are empowered to take an active role in their healthcare journey. You can **browse detailed profiles of our doctors** and their specialties. To **securely book an appointment and manage your health records**, a quick and easy **account creation is required**. Once registered, you gain full access to scheduling, viewing past visits, and accessing your health data anytime, anywhere.
